Conference Report: 2017 International Conference on Matrix Inequalities and Matrix Equations

      The 2017 International Conference on Matrix Inequalities and Matrix Equations (MIME2017) took place on June 6-8, 2017 at New Lehu Hotel of Shanghai University. This is the third serial conference hosted at Shanghai University. More than a hundred faculty members and graduate students from Belarus, Canada, Japan, Portugal, Russia, Serbia, the USA and mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan attended the conference. The conference was sponsored by Shanghai University, the International Research Center for Tensor and Matrix Theory (IRCTMT) and Gaoyuan Discipline of Shanghai.

      The scientific committee of conference consists of
Delin Chu, National University of Singapore, Singapore
Alexander E. Guterman, Moscow State University, Russia
Chi-Kwong Li, College of William and Mary, USA
Tin-Yau Tam, Auburn University, USA
Qing-Wen Wang, Shanghai University, China
Fuzhen Zhang, Nova Southeastern University, USA
Yang Zhang, University of Manitoba, Canada.

      The conference has attracted a few leading experts of the area to share their exciting research. There were 40 talks arranged in the 3-day conference. Conference pictures and conference program are available at the website: http://math.shu.edu.cn/mime2017/

      The next MIME conference will be held at Shanghai University on June 7-10, 2018 (June 7 is the registration date), we expect to see you then.
